They then chained the bank's safe to the vehicle, apparently to drag it away.They didn't get far, however.Alerted by reports of the equipment theft, police in the town of Oaxtepec used surveillance cameras to locate the men and detect their noisy, slow-motion robbery attempt early Friday.One suspect was detained, while the others fled.Advertisement
