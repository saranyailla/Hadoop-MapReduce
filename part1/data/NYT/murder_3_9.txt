Other videos show him pretending to dig a grave for Baskin and threatening to mail her rattlesnakes.His attorneys say the videos weren't meant to be taken seriously.Maldonado-Passage is also accused of killing five tigers in October 2017 and selling and offering to sell tiger cubs in violation of the Endangered Species Act.Advertisement
