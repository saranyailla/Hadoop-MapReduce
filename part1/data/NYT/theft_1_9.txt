AdvertisementSupported byBy The Associated PressMEMPHIS, Tenn. — Police in Tennessee say an 11-year-old boy now faces a murder charge after a teenager he is accused of shooting died.The Memphis Police Department said Sunday that a 14-year-old boy has died after he was shot Wednesday night. Police said the teen had been hospitalized in extremely critical condition before his death.Police said a 34-year-old man also was wounded in the shooting. He is in critical but stable condition.Police are not identifying the 11-year-old. He is charged with first-degree murder, murder during a theft, and attempted murder. A judge will decide at a hearing whether the boy will be charged as an adult. It was not clear Sunday night if he had a lawyer.
