LAREDO, TX (KGNS) - A man accused of sexually assaulting a minor is arrested by police.According to Laredo Police, 49-year-old Roberto Silva was arrested for an incident that happened on December 14th of last year.Investigators with the Laredo Police Department Special Investigation Unit launched an investigation after a report was made that Silva allegedly assaulted a 14-year-old while giving her a ride to school.According to the complaint, Silva allegedly touched the girl’s privates over her clothing.After a thorough investigation, the District Attorney’s Office determined there was enough evidence to warrant an arrest.Silva was charged with indecency with a child through sexual contact.
