JACKSONVILLE, Fla. - A Crime Stoppers tip landed a suspected masked robber behind bars, the Jacksonville Sheriff's Office said.
                     Police released surveillance images Saturday of a masked man accused in a holdup at a Northside business. Police did not say where the robbery happened or when it happened.
                                  After a tip was called in, Kenneth Kenny, 53, was identified as the man in the photos and was arrested, police said.
                     
Mask wearing robbery suspect arrested. Thank you to the tipster that called Crime Stoppers. That tip led to the location and ID of suspect Kenneth Kenny, 53.
