Lincoln Woman’s Club will hold a tea at 1 p.m. Wednesday, April 17. The program will feature Dr. Dennis Campbell, who is the Director of Creekside. Carol Mills will give devotions and Rita Wilson and Marta McCall will serve as greeters. McCall will also run the 50/50. Carolyn Wessels, Carol Schwantz, Lynn Corley and Sue Beaver will serve as hostesses. Ladies are asked to wear blue to honor child abuse prevention.
