    Tariq Zehawi, NorthJerseyAuthorities are investigating a shooting in Paterson early Saturday morning that wounded a 27-year-old city man. At approximately 12:14 a.m., Paterson police responded to a report of shots fired in the area of 210 Walnut St., according to a press release from the Passaic County Prosecutors Office.Officers arriving on the scene found the victim in the street, who was then transported to Saint Joseph’s Regional Medical Center in Paterson for treatment, according to authorities.The investigation remains active and ongoing.
