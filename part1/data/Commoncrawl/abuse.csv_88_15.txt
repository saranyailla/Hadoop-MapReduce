CHILLIWACK, B.C.: APRIL 15, 2019 – A B.C.-wide arrest warrant has been issued for Downie Dee Maccallum.

HANDOUT / CHILLIWACK RCMP / PNG 
A provincewide arrest warrant has been issued for a Chilliwack woman in a stolen-property case.Downie Dee Maccallum, 33, of Chilliwack was arrested April 1 when a Chilliwack RCMP officer spotted a stolen vehicle parked in a dirt lot.Officers who arrived at the scene arrested three individuals who were sitting inside the car, among them Maccallum.She was then charged with possession of stolen property and released from custody. She is expected back in court this month.Maccallum is described as white, about 5-foot-5 and weighing 150 pounds.
