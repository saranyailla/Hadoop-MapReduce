TRINITY — A man who was allegedly found in possession of methamphetamine is in custody after being linked to a trailer theft Saturday.According to the Randolph County Sheriff’s Office, deputies responded to a call regarding a stolen utility trailer on Ridge Drive in Trinity and encountered two suspects, a man and a woman.Investigation led to methamphetamine being located in the pair’s vehicle, as well as in the man’s pocket, the press release stated.Cody William Toler, 31, 1909 Henley Country Road, Randleman, and Heather Marie Cunningham, 32, 5240 Ridge Drive, Trinity, were arrested, taken to the Randolph County Jail and issued $4,000 secured bonds after being charged with felony possession of methamphetamine and possession of drug paraphernalia.A couple of hours following the incident, the sheriff’s office was contacted by the Archdale Police Department who advised that the trailer had just been reported stolen in their jurisdiction.The trailer was returned to the owner, and Toler received an additional $10,000 secured bond after being served with an additional charge for felony possession of stolen goods/property.During the investigation, information was received that Toler recently sold a trailer and lawn mower to an unknown individual. The property is believed to be stolen.Anyone with information is urged to contact Randolph County Sheriff’s Office at 336-318-6698, 911, or the Randolph County Crime Stoppers tip line at (336-672-CRIME or 7463).
