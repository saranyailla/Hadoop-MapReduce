A 39-year-old man faces charges after a clerk at a Dundas Street convenience store was threatened with a knife Sunday night, police say.The suspect walked out with two containers of mint chocolate chip ice cream around 10:15 p.m., whipping out a knife when he was confronted by a clerk, London police said in a media release.Officers tracked down the suspect nearby.A London man is charged with robbery and resisting arrest.
