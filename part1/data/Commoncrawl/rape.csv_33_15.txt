Town council meets Tuesday afternoon and a number of items are on the agenda. A representative from Delshen Therapeutics will be on hand to speak with council members.Kirkland Lake’s pigeon problem will also be, once again, on the agenda as will the proposed taxi cab licensing bylaw.The meeting will take place in council chambers at town hall beginning at 4:40 pm.
