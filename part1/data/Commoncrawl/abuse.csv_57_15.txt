By Jacob Pucci | jpucci@syracuse.com ROME, N.Y. - A 14-year-old boy has been arrested on charges that he sexually abused a 13-year-old girl on a school bus in the city of Rome. According to the Oneida County Sheriff’s Office, the boy touched the girl’s “intimate body parts” aboard the bus in late February. The Oneida County Child Advocacy Center launched an investigation on March 7 after being alerted to the incident by Rome police. The boy, who was not identified, was charged with second-degree sexual abuse and endangering the welfare of a child, both misdemeanors. He will appear in juvenile probation at a later date, deputies said. An order of protection was requested on behalf of the victim.
