DOTHAN, AL (WSFA) - The Dothan Police Department was involved in a vehicle pursuit of a suspected homicide suspect out of North Carolina Saturday.According to police, the suspect was located in a stolen vehicle where police attempted to conduct a traffic stop.The driver fled and led police on a chase that traveled around Ross Clark Circle to Montgomery Highway where the suspect turned onto Cherokee Avenue before losing control and crashing into a tree.The susect was taken to the hospital where he is receiving treatment.Police say no other drivers were injured in the chase.
