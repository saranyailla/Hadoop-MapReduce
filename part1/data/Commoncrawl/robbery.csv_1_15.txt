

KITCHENER — Two men were arrested at a Kitchener motel in connection with a robbery at the CIBC branch in the downtown last week.The Kitchener men, aged 35 and 27, are charged with robbery with a firearm and robbery after the CIBC branch at King and Queen streets was robbed on April 10 just before 4 p.m.Waterloo Regional Police previously said a man entered the bank with handgun and left with an undisclosed amount of cash. No one was hurt during the robbery.Police spokesperson Const. Ashley Dietrich said police continue to investigate the other eight bank robberies in the region in recent months.Anyone with information about this robbery is asked to contact police at 519-570-9777 or Crime Stoppers at 1-800-222-8477.
