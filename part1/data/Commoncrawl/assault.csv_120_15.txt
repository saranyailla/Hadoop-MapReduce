Police say others were involved.On April 9, a woman living in the 1000 block of South White Station near Park told police someone had shot into her house while her son and brother were inside. Police found eight shell casings.Witnesses on the scene said the shooter took off in a black sedan after firing shots at the home.The next day, police responded to a shooting at the same address. The woman told police she was sleeping inside at the time.There was no word on what led up to the shootings.Filed in: News Topics: east memphis, shooting News, weather, and sports headlines from our team
