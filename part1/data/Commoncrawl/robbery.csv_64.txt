                                        Simi Valley Police Department (Photo: STAR FILE PHOTO)A 37-year-old Simi Valley man was arrested Friday after police said they tracked down the car stereo he allegedly stole when it was listed on a secondhand sales app. The victim’s car stereo was stolen late Thursday or early Friday while their vehicle was parked near Yosemite and Los Angeles Avenues, Simi Valley police said. Several hours later, the victim notified police they saw what appeared to be the stolen car stereo on an app called Let’s Go, authorities said. Authorities contacted the seller online and set up a time to meet him to buy the stereo, police said. The suspect was met with officers and arrested on suspicion of possession of stolen property. The $700 stereo was returned to the victim, police said. Read more:
